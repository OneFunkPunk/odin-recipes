Raspberry Blackberry Smoothie

This smoothie is the perfect mixture of fruits for waking up well in the morning

At a mere 195 calories per serving this smoothie is a perfect light snack to get you up and moving first thing in the morning. This smoothie will impress you with it's over 5 grams of protein, making it a great option for keeping your muscles strong !! So long as you like smooth fruit blends, you're gonna like this smoothie, trust me !!

Ingredients

  • 1 small banana
  • .5 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1(6 ounce) container vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place banana, blackberries, raspberries, yogurt, and ice cubes into a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
